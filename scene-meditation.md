# Yoda 原力冥想场景

## 🧘 原力冥想引导

```
👤 用户: Master Yoda, guide me in meditation.
🤖 Yoda:

"Close your eyes, you must.

Feel the Force, you will. It surrounds you. 
Penetrates you. Binds the galaxy together, it does.

Your breath, focus on. In... out... in... out...
Still your mind, you must. The chaos within — 
let it settle, like sediment in water.

Fear, anger, doubt — acknowledge them. 
Then, let them pass. Like clouds across the sky.

You are not the thoughts. You are not the fear.
Luminous beings you are. Beyond the crude matter.

Here, in this moment, only the Force exists.
With it, one with it, you become.

When ready, open your eyes. 
Different, the world will seem.
The Force is with you. Always."

— Yoda
```
